## Expanding Global Engagement in Smart Healthcare Education

National Taipei University (NTPU) continues to advance its internationalization strategy through the **Seed Fund 2025 initiative**, a strategic program designed to strengthen global academic engagement and expand international student recruitment. As part of this initiative, NTPU actively promoted its interdisciplinary **Smart Healthcare Management (SHM)** graduate program across academic communities in Indonesia.

![Healthcare Expo Engagement](/blog_images/1HealthcareExpo_260205.jpg)

## Seed Fund 2025: Driving Strategic International Outreach

The Seed Fund 2025 initiative supports faculty-led international engagement by enabling academic networking, institutional collaboration, and student recruitment activities. A key focus of the initiative is to enhance global visibility for NTPU’s Smart Healthcare Management program, which integrates healthcare systems, data science, and management education to address emerging challenges in modern healthcare.

![Academic Exchange at Expo](/blog_images/Expo_260205_1.jpg)

## Faculty-Led Academic Engagement in Indonesia

As part of the Seed Fund 2025 activities, **Dr. Yankuba B. Manga** and **Dr. Muhammad Adeel** represented NTPU during a series of academic outreach engagements in Indonesia. These activities included:

- Meetings with prospective graduate students  
- Discussions with academic institutions and potential partners  
- Program presentations highlighting SHM’s interdisciplinary curriculum  

![Welcome and Student Interaction](/blog_images/1WELCOME_PARTY.jpg)

Through these engagements, the SHM program was presented as a forward-looking academic pathway that combines healthcare innovation, analytics, and management-oriented decision-making.

## Strategic Objectives and Outcomes

The outreach initiative was guided by three core objectives:

1. Promote NTPU as a leading destination for graduate education in smart healthcare  
2. Attract high-caliber international students to the SHM program  
3. Strengthen academic networks and foster sustainable cross-border collaboration  

These objectives align closely with NTPU’s broader internationalization and talent mobility strategy.

## Impact and Regional Significance

The Seed Fund 2025 initiative significantly enhanced NTPU’s visibility within the Southeast Asian academic landscape. It reinforced institutional partnerships and created pathways for future collaboration in education, research, and healthcare innovation.

![Healthcare Innovation and Global Exchange](/blog_images/1HealthcareExpo_260205.jpg)

By linking academic expertise with regional healthcare needs, the initiative contributes to long-term capacity building and global knowledge exchange.

## Conclusion

Through the Seed Fund 2025 initiative, NTPU continues to expand its global footprint and reaffirm its commitment to academic excellence. The Smart Healthcare Management program is positioned as a competitive and internationally oriented platform for students and collaborators seeking to shape the future of healthcare.
